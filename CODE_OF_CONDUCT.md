# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to communicate clearly, stay respectful, and focus on improving reusable office automation templates in a constructive way.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and direct
- give actionable feedback
- keep discussions technical and collaborative
- avoid sharing private credentials, tenant-specific secrets, or sensitive internal material

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- hostile or discriminatory language
- publishing private organizational data
- encouraging unsafe automation behavior without warning

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private communication channel with the repository maintainer instead of posting sensitive details publicly.
